## Critical Anti-Patterns

| Pattern | Description | Severity | Prevention Mechanism |
|---------|-------------|----------|---------------------|
| 1:1-meters rendering | Rendering bodies at true 1:1 meters forced `Camera3D.far = 1e12`, which broke Godot's light culler (`prepare_camera: "!res"`) and destroyed depth precision | advisory | Render in scaled UNIT-space: `ToLocalDoubleUnits(ship) × RenderFactor[ship space]`, camera far `1e6`. Do NOT reintroduce a large far plane or "render meters" radius path. |
| Body-scale unit basis | Using each body's own `Scale` as the render unit basis is inconsistent across mixed-scale bodies in one frame and mismatches the radius transform | advisory | `ToLocalDoubleUnits` divides by the OBSERVER (ship) scale; radii use `radiusMeters / ship.LocalPos.Scale × factor`. Keep one basis per frame. |
| Parent-body frame mismatch | `parent.LocalPos` is in the GRANDPARENT frame; diffing it against `ship.LocalPos` (PARENT frame) placed the planet ~1 AU away | advisory | Render the parent at `-ship.LocalPos` (frame origin); render siblings via `ToLocalDoubleUnits`. Preserve both paths in RenderBridge. |

_All three were discovered through failure during Wave 1 and are already FIXED. They are advisory so a resuming agent does not undo them._
