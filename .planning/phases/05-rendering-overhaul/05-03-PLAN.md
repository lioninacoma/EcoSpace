<objective>
Narrow the post-process pass to its correct screen-space job and complete the continuous star + galaxy handoffs. Strip the galaxy loop and galaxy uniforms out of `luminous_pass.gdshader` and `LuminousPassRenderer` (galaxies live in the sky shader now), relax the `sky_pixel` depth gate for near stars so the glow halo wraps the visible star mesh (`is_near = star_lod_weights[i]`, contribution scaled by `lod_fade = 1.0 - LodWeight`), and extend `LuminousLod.GalaxyDiscWeight`'s fade band past the galaxy SOI boundary so the sky-shader disc fades in gradually instead of popping. Play-test Star + Galaxy/Universe space.

Purpose: Deliver the three-stage star handoff (D-11) — FAR point → MID point-fades/glow-grows → NEAR mesh+glow — and the galaxy far-disc ↔ resolved-stars crossfade (D-13), both pop-free by construction because every drawer reads the same descriptor. Fixes the "galaxy pops out of nowhere" bug traced to the GalaxyDiscWeight band topping out below the SOI boundary.
Output: A near-star-glow-only post-process pass with a LOD-relaxed halo gate; a star-only LuminousPassRenderer; and an extended galaxy fade band that removes the pop.
</objective>
