Re-establish the Sky-shader skybox as the descriptor-fed distant-body drawer (D-07 REVERSED) and fix near-star findability. Reset `SkyboxRenderer`'s disabled `process_mode` in Main.tscn, refeed `SkyboxRenderer.SyncSkyPoints` from `LuminousDescriptorBuilder.Descriptors[]` (dropping its own classify+project loop), add a display-only brightness floor so distant stars stay findable (closes P1), add the `galaxy_disc_weights` uniform groundwork to `skybox.gdshader`, and fix the 05-02 missing-sun regression by flooring the near-star mesh emissive energy in `WorldRenderer.RenderBodyAt` (D-12).

This plan makes the skybox consume the Plan-1 descriptor as its single feed and restores the near sun as a real bright sphere mesh — the two findability foundations the rest of the phase builds on.
Output: A running, descriptor-fed skybox; an always-visible distant-star floor; a visibly bright near sun; and the galaxy_disc_weights uniform groundwork for Plan 3's crossfade. No new node types; no topology change to SyncBodies.
</objective>
