<objective>
Build the depth-aware post-process luminous pass: a `shader_type spatial` quad (`luminous_pass.gdshader`) hosted by `LuminousPassRenderer` as a child of Camera3D, consuming the Plan-1 descriptor. Near stars get glow/halo; distant stars are points; the point↔mesh handoff is driven by the descriptor's continuous `LodWeight` so it blends seamlessly over the existing sphere meshes (depth-aware). This supersedes the Sky-shader STAR POINTS while the skybox stays running as a fallback. Play-test Star and Galaxy space.

Purpose: Deliver the headline star-LOD vision (mesh+glow near ↔ point far, one continuous distance blend) and close P1 (galaxy-space star findability) via the always-visible point/glow floor — all guaranteed pop-free by the shared descriptor (D-02).
Output: `LuminousPassRenderer` + `luminous_pass.gdshader` wired into Main.tscn under Camera3D; star points/glow drawn in the 3D transparent pass before the dither.
</objective>
