<objective>
Build the headline structural fix: a unified, always-on MultiMesh PSF star-point renderer
(D-56/D-57) plus its additive quad shader (D-53/D-55). Every `Type.Star` body becomes a
brightness-floored point at every tier, fed per-frame from `UniObject` + `StarRendering`
under floating origin, so sub-pixel galaxy-space stars are never invisible. The sphere mesh
(WorldRenderer, unchanged) layers additively on top as true distance shrinks — the continuous
blend that makes the skybox↔mesh handoff (RND-07) a grow-in, not a pop.

Purpose: Closes the P1 `galaxy-space-star-meshes-invisible` root cause (sub-pixel radius +
~0 emission at light-year distance) structurally, and re-enables UAT Tests 2/4/6.
Output: `Scripts/Render/StarPointRenderer.cs` (new), `Shaders/star_point.gdshader` (new),
Main.tscn node mount.
</objective>
