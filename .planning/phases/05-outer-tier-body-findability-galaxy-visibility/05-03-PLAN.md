<objective>
Rework the HUD target circle to be UniObject-driven (D-50, refines the Phase-4 D-46 circle):
both its screen POSITION (`UniMath.RelativePosition` ship→body in the LCA frame → camera
projection) and its SIZE (`StarRendering.AngularRadius` with the D-46 min on-screen radius floor)
come from the body's configured `UniObject`, NOT from `WorldRenderer.GetRenderPosition`/
`GetRenderRadius`. This makes the circle work UNIFORMLY for sky-only galaxies (no mesh),
sub-pixel galaxy-space stars, and planets alike — so "you can target it but the circle marks
black space" becomes "the circle hugs a visible body" (D-58 consistency watch item). The HUD
adaptive-distance readout (D-10, already reaches ly) supplies the D-51 distance cue.

Purpose: Closes the targeting half of the P2 gap; makes findability generalize to every target.
Output: Hud.UpdateTargetCircle reworked; WorldRenderer.RenderFactorFor made accessible.
</objective>
