## Critical Anti-Patterns (do NOT repeat these)
- [ANTI-PATTERN]: Authoring positions in a body's unit-scale and passing them to an
  API that expects metres → off by the scale factor (1e16). Mitigation: always pass
  metres to `AddGameObject`; let `UniVec3`/`Normalize` derive the integer Units.
- [ANTI-PATTERN]: Building a sky-disc tangent basis from `disc_normal` (degenerate
  when ∥ world-up) → ring/NaN. Mitigation: basis from the view direction + front-
  hemisphere gate; verify shaders in-game (build/tests can't catch GDShader bugs).
